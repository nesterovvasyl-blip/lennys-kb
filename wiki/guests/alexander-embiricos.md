# Alexander Embiricos

> Product Lead for Codex at OpenAI, former founder and PM at Dropbox.

## Content

Alexander Embiricos leads Codex at OpenAI, OpenAI's coding agent. Before OpenAI, he founded a screen-sharing pair-programming startup and was a PM at Dropbox.

### Key Ideas

- **Codex as software engineering teammate**: Not just a coding tool — it participates in planning, writing, testing, deploying, and maintaining code. North star is proactive, autonomous action.
- **Any capable AI agent should be a coding agent**: Writing code is the most reliable and composable way for AI to use computers and take real-world actions.
- **Human typing speed is the bottleneck**: The underappreciated limiting factor to AGI productivity is not model intelligence but human review throughput.
- **Meet users where they are**: Codex's 20x growth came from pulling back from purely cloud-based async and integrating into IDEs and CLIs, letting users build trust incrementally.
- **Distribution > building in AI era**: As building gets cheaper (Sora app: #1 in 28 days, 2-3 engineers), deep customer understanding becomes the primary moat.
- **Bottoms-up culture at OpenAI**: Fast experiments over top-down roadmaps, letting the market reveal what works.

### Notable Quotes

> "If you want to build any agent, maybe you should be building a coding agent."

> "If I could only choose one thing to understand, it would be really meaningful understanding of the problems that a certain customer has."
