# Adam Grenier

> Growth marketing leader, former Head of Growth Marketing at Uber and VP Marketing at MasterClass.

## Content

Adam Grenier built Uber's growth marketing infrastructure from scratch as Head of Growth Marketing and Innovation. He later served as VP of Product and Marketing at Lambda School and VP of Marketing at MasterClass. He now advises and invests in startups.

### Key Ideas

- **Emerging Channel Evaluation**: Three-ingredient framework — (1) overlap of customer needs, company goals, and channel strengths; (2) channel DNA (trajectory, risk, monetization); (3) company DNA (risk appetite, team capacity, channel mix).
- **Assume lost PMF in downturns**: When economic conditions shift, the customer base changes fundamentally. Start over on product-market fit rather than tweaking the margins.
- **Growth CMO**: A new marketing leader archetype — data-driven, agile, tightly integrated with product, willing to experiment across all functions.
- **New channels rarely work**: Brand new channels succeed only ~5% of the time. Cap exploration at one quarter.
- **Improv principles in business**: "Yes, and..." and the "gift of details" improve cross-functional collaboration and keep teams building on ideas rather than shutting them down.

### Notable Quotes

> "Start by assuming you no longer have product market fit, because you had product market fit in a different market."

> "Brand spanking new channels, I don't know, they rarely work or are worth the effort early that you hope that they will be."
