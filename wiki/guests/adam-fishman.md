# Adam Fishman

> Growth and product leader who was the first growth hire at Lyft and led product/growth at Patreon.

## Content

Adam Fishman was the first growth and marketing hire at Lyft, then led product and growth at Patreon for four years, followed by CPO at Imperfect Foods. He's currently an EIR at Reforge and writes the FishmanAF Newsletter.

### Key Ideas

- **Onboarding is the #1 lever**: It's the only product experience 100% of users touch. Optimizing Patreon's onboarding improved creator first/second-month revenue by 25%.
- **Growth Competency Model**: Four quadrants for hiring growth talent — Growth Execution, Customer Knowledge, Growth Strategy, Communication & Influence. Build balanced teams, don't chase unicorns.
- **Opinionated Defaults**: Make it hard to do the wrong thing and easy to do the right thing in onboarding. Preserve user choice while guiding toward success based on data from best performers.
- **Optimize onboarding for retention over conversion**: Adding strategic friction weeds out poor-fit users, raising cohort retention by 10–20 points even if initial conversion dips.
- **PMF Framework for Candidates**: People, Mission, Financials — settling for 2 out of 3 consistently leads to bad outcomes.

### Notable Quotes

> "Onboarding is the only part of your product experience that a hundred percent of people are ever going to touch."

> "Your brand is the promise that you're making and your product experience is your delivery of that promise."
