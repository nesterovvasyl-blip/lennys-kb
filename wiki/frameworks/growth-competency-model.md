# Growth Competency Model

> A four-quadrant model for hiring and evaluating growth talent — build balanced teams, don't chase unicorns.

## Content

The Growth Competency Model maps growth skills across four quadrants to help leaders assess candidates, identify team gaps, and structure hiring:

**1. Growth Execution**
- Channel fluency (SEO, paid, referral, lifecycle, etc.)
- Experimentation discipline (hypothesis, design, analysis)
- Speed of iteration

**2. Customer Knowledge**
- Data fluency (can read and query product analytics)
- User psychology (understands motivation, friction, and behavior)
- Qualitative research skills

**3. Growth Strategy**
- Loop modeling (identifies and designs compounding growth loops)
- Prioritization (knows where to invest for highest leverage)
- Systems thinking

**4. Communication & Influence**
- Stakeholder management (partners effectively across functions)
- Leadership (can drive alignment without direct authority)
- Storytelling with data

### Key insight

No single person excels in all four quadrants — the "unicorn" growth hire is a myth. The goal is to build a **balanced team** where the collective coverage is strong, not to find one person who does everything. Use the model to diagnose team gaps and make targeted hires.
