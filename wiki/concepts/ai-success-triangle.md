# AI Success Triangle

> Three organizational conditions that predict successful AI adoption.

## Content

The AI Success Triangle identifies three conditions that consistently separate companies that succeed with AI from those that fail, based on 50+ AI deployments observed by [[aishwarya-naresh-reganti-kiriti-badam]]:

1. **Hands-on humble leaders** — Leaders who roll up their sleeves, understand the technology at a working level, and don't delegate AI strategy entirely to vendors or consultants.

2. **Empowering culture** — Teams that see AI as augmentation, not replacement. Fear of job loss causes employees to sandbaag adoption. Successful organizations explicitly frame AI as a tool that makes people more effective.

3. **Technical obsession with workflows** — 80% of successful AI builders spend the majority of their time understanding existing workflows deeply before building. The insight is that AI success is workflow-first, not model-first.
